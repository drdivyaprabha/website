Why choose Homoeopathy?

Homoeopathy is a holistic system of medicine that treats the individual as a whole, aiming to stimulate the body's innate healing abilities. Unlike conventional medicine, which often focuses on suppressing symptoms, homoeopathy seeks to address the underlying causes of illness and restore balance to the body, mind, and spirit.

How does Homoeopathy work?

Homoeopathy is believed to work by stimulating the body's vital force to restore balance and health. Remedies are prepared through a process of serial dilution and succussion (vigorous shaking), which is thought to enhance the healing properties of the substance while minimizing any potential toxicity.

How long does it take to see results with homeopathy?

The timeline for experiencing results with homoeopathy can vary depending on the individual, the condition being treated, and other factors such as overall health and lifestyle. Some people may notice improvements shortly after starting treatment, while others may require longer-term care to see significant changes. Patience and consistent follow-up with a homoeopathic practitioner are key.
